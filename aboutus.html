About us

We are a family orientated business with a strong work ethic and an eye for detail. our two directors have 20 years combined experience as fixers, over the years our combined skill sets have broadened our range of knowledge and skills as we have learned and implemented many skills around our trade, from SFS to dry lining to rain screen cladding. We operate from West Berkshire but we will travel anywhere in the UK, dedicated to providing a fast, professional service every time.

We have several IPAF trained operatives with power assisted licences, including both our directors and we have recently become CITB (construction industry training board) certified. We are dedicated to getting our hard working team the accreditation and training they need and deserve, we are also looking into getting young people into work with apprentice schemes, with potential opportunities for hard working & committed young people who are looking into construction as a career, but not the traditional, conventional building trades.
