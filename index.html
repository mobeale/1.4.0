We are CRS.

Welcome to Cold Rolled Solutions Limited. We are a CITB certified company based in Berkshire that specialises in steel framing systems; including infill, free standing, load bearing and pre fabricated panels.

We are also highly skilled in dry lining and ceilings, our directors have over 20 years combined experience in commercial and residential interior works, specifically with ceilings, metal framing/gridding (including sub-grids above suspended ceilings) and pan tiles.
